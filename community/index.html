Collaborate

The ManageIQ Depot is a place for sharing and downloading extensions such as policies and reports.

In addition, you don't have to be a developer to be able to contribute. Filing issues/bugs is a great way to help. The ManageIQ project also welcomes translations!

Let’s Meet

Here are some opportunities to meet with members of the community to learn and share about ManageIQ, whether online or in person, at international conferences or local meetups. Materials from past events will be made available here as well.

Sprint reviews

There is a ManageIQ Sprint review every 2 weeks open to the community. To join the online video conference, please use this Bluejeans link.

You can import the ManageIQ community calendar to be notified about ManageIQ events and Sprint reviews.
